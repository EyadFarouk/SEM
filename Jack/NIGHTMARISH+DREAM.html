NIGHTMARISH DREAM

NIGHTMARISH DREAM

I was desperate and rusting
So I loved every part of you.
I was hopeless and trusting
So I fell for every word you said.

In the end it was all a game
Administrator, you controlled everything.
In the end, it was all just fake
Virtual Reality; you pulled me into it
Beguile, I admit.

So cheaply, you sold me
So confidently, you tricked me
So genuinely, you lied to me
So determined, you opened me
You filled my lungs
Only to take away the air.
I surrendered my happiness to you
I surrendered my future to you
I surrendered my heart to you
But you knew they weren't of value.

A bright star, I observed you
Your glow, your illumination.
Darkening down, you left me
Like a supernova through my heart,
A Black Hole tearing it apart.

The fort had to be fortified,
More walls, large and slight
Skyscrapers, huge and wide.
A heart's confirmation to pass, so clever
But the heart has died
Now the gates are locked away forever.
